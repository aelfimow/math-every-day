\section{Exercise 773}

Given:
\[
\lim_{x\to 0}{\frac{x}{\sin{3x}}}
\]

Solution:
\[
u(x) = x
\]
\[
u'(x) = 1
\]
\[
u'(0) = 1
\]

\[
v(x) = \sin{3x}
\]
\[
v'(x) = 3\cos{3x}
\]
\[
v'(0) = 3\cos{3\cdot 0} = 3\cos{0} = 3\cdot 1 = 3
\]

\[
\lim_{x\to 0}{\frac{x}{\sin{3x}}} = \lim_{x\to 0}{\frac{u'(x)}{v'(x)}} = \frac{1}{3}
\]

Result:
\[
\lim_{x\to 0}{\frac{x}{\sin{3x}}} = \frac{1}{3}
\]
