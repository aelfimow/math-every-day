\section{Exercise 1003a}

Given:
\[
y = x - \coth{x}
\]

Solution:
\[
y = 1 - \left(-\frac{1}{\sinh^{2}{x}}\right) = 1 + \frac{1}{\sinh^{2}{x}} = \frac{\sinh^{2}{x} + 1}{\sinh^{2}{x}} =
\]
\[
\frac{\cosh^{2}}{\sinh^{2}{x}} = \coth^{2}{x}
\]

Result:
\[
y' = \coth^{2}{x}
\]
