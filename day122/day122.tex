\section{Exercise}

Given: $a = 294$ and $b = 201$

Solution:
\[
    294 = 1\cdot 201 + 93
\]
\[
    201 = 2\cdot 93 + 15
\]
\[
    93 = 6\cdot 15 + 3
\]
\[
    15 = 5\cdot 3
\]
\[
    \gcd(a, b) = 3
\]

Result:
\[
    \gcd(a, b) = 3
\]
