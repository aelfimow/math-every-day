\section{Exercises}

Given: $a = 1071$ and $b = 1029$

Solution:
\[
1071 = 1\cdot 1029 + 42
\]
\[
1029 = 24\cdot 42 + 21
\]
\[
42 = 2\cdot 21 + 0
\]

Result:
\[
\gcd(1071, 1029) = 21
\]

Given: $a = 54$ and $b = 24$

Solution:
\[
54 = 2\cdot 24 + 6
\]
\[
24 = 4\cdot 6 + 0
\]

Result:
\[
\gcd(54, 24) = 6
\]
