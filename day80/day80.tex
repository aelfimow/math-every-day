\section{Exercise 855a}

Given:
\[
y = \frac{1}{2x^{2}} - \frac{1}{3x^{3}}
\]

Solution:
\[
y = \frac{1}{2x^{2}} - \frac{1}{3x^{3}} = \frac{1}{2}x^{-2} - \frac{1}{3}x^{-3}
\]

\[
y' = \frac{1}{2}\cdot(-2)x^{-2 - 1} - \frac{1}{3}\cdot(-3)x^{-3 - 1} = -x^{-3} + x^{-4} =
\]
\[
x^{-4} - x^{-3} = \frac{1}{x^{4}} - \frac{1}{x^{3}} = \frac{1 - x}{x^{4}}
\]

Result:
\[
y' = \frac{1 - x}{x^{4}}
\]
