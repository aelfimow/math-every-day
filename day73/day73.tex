\section{Exercise 892a}

Given:
\[
y = a\sqrt{\cos{2\phi}}
\]

Solution:
\[
y' = a\frac{(-\sin{2\phi})2}{2\sqrt{\cos{2\phi}}} = -a\frac{\sin{2\phi}}{\sqrt{\cos{2\phi}}}
\]

Result:
\[
y' = -a\frac{\sin{2\phi}}{\sqrt{\cos{2\phi}}}
\]
