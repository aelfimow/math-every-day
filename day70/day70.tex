\section{Exercise 881}

Given:
\[
y = \sin^{3}{x} + \cos^{3}{x}
\]

Solution:
\[
y' = 3\sin^{2}{x}\cos{x} + 3\cos^{2}{x}(-\sin{x}) = 3(\sin^{2}{x}\cos{x} - \cos^{2}{x}\sin{x})
\]

Result:
\[
y' = 3(\sin^{2}{x}\cos{x} - \cos^{2}{x}\sin{x})
\]
