\section{Exercise 68.2a}

Given:
\[f(x) = \frac{-4}{x^{2}}\]

Solution:
\[f(x) = \frac{-4}{x^{2}} = -4x^{-2}\]
\[f'(x) = -4\cdot(-2)x^{-2 - 1} = 8x^{-3} = \frac{8}{x^{3}}\]

Result:
\[f'(x) = \frac{8}{x^{3}}\]
