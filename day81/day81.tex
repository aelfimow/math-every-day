\section{Exercise 856a}

Given:
\[
y = x - \sin{x}
\]

Solution:
\[
y' = 1 - \cos{x}
\]

Using:
\[
\sin^{2}{x} = \frac{1}{2}(1 - \cos{2x})
\]
\[
2\sin^{2}{x} = 1 - \cos{2x}
\]

\[
y' = 1 - \cos{x} = 2\sin^{2}{\frac{x}{2}}
\]

Result:
\[
y' = 2\sin^{2}{\frac{x}{2}}
\]
