\section{Exercise 68.3a}

Given:
\[f(x) = 2\sqrt{x}\]

Solution:
\[f(x) = 2\sqrt{x} = 2x^{\frac{1}{2}}\]

\[f'(x) = 2\cdot\frac{1}{2}\cdot x^{\frac{1}{2} - 1}\]
\[f'(x) = x^{-\frac{1}{2}} = \frac{1}{x^\frac{1}{2}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\]

Result:
\[f'(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\]
