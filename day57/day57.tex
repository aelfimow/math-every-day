\section{Exercise 594}

Given:
\[
\left|
    \begin{array}{ccc}
    1 & b & 1\\
    0 & b & 0\\
    b & 0 & -b
    \end{array}
\right|
\]

Solution:
\[
\left|
    \begin{array}{ccc}
    1 & b & 1\\
    0 & b & 0\\
    b & 0 & -b
    \end{array}
\right| =
\left|
    \begin{array}{ccc}
    0 & b & 0\\
    1 & b & 1\\
    b & 0 & -b
    \end{array}
\right| =
\]
\[
 -b(1(-b) - 1b) = -b(-b - b) = -b(-2b) = 2b^{2}
\]

Result:
\[
\left|
    \begin{array}{ccc}
    1 & b & 1\\
    0 & b & 0\\
    b & 0 & -b
    \end{array}
\right| = 2b^{2}
\]
