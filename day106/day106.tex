\section{Exercise 1021a}

Given:
\[
y = \sin^{2}{x}
\]

Solution:
\[
y' = 2\sin{x}\cos{x}
\]
\[
y'' = 2(\cos{x}\cos{x} + \sin{x}(-\sin{x})) = 2(\cos{x}\cos{x} - \sin{x}\sin{x}) = 2\cos{(x + x)} = 2\cos{2x}
\]

Result:
\[
y'' = 2\cos{2x}
\]
