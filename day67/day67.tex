\section{Exercise 875a}

Given:
\[
y = \sin{\frac{x}{2}} + \cos{\frac{x}{2}}
\]

Solution:
\[
y' = \frac{1}{2}\cos{\frac{x}{2}} + (-\frac{1}{2}\sin{\frac{x}{2}}) = \frac{1}{2}(\cos{\frac{x}{2}} - \frac{1}{2}\sin{\frac{x}{2}})
\]

Result:
\[
y' = \frac{1}{2}(\cos{\frac{x}{2}} - \frac{1}{2}\sin{\frac{x}{2}})
\]
