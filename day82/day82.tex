\section{Exercise 857a}

Given:
\[
y = x^{2}\cos{x}
\]

Solution:
\[
y' = 2x\cos{x} + x^{2}(-\sin{x}) = x(2\cos{x} - x\sin{x})
\]

Result:
\[
y' = x(2\cos{x} - x\sin{x})
\]
