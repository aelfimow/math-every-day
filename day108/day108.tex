\section{Exercise 1023a}

Given:
\[
y = x\ln{x}
\]

Solution:
\[
y' = \ln{x} + x\frac{1}{x} = \ln{x} + 1
\]
\[
y'' = \frac{1}{x} = x^{-1}
\]
\[
y''' = (-1)x^{-1 - 1} = -x^{-2} = -\frac{1}{x^{2}}
\]

Result:
\[
y''' = -\frac{1}{x^{2}}
\]
