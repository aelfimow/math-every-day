\section{Exercise 598}

Given:
\[
\left|
    \begin{array}{ccc}
    12 & 6 & -4\\
    6 & 4 & 4\\
    3 & 2 & 8
    \end{array}
\right|
\]

Solution:
\[
12\cdot4\cdot8 + 6\cdot4\cdot3 + (-4)\cdot6\cdot2 -
(3\cdot4\cdot(-4) + 2\cdot4\cdot12 + 8\cdot6\cdot6) =
\]
\[
384 + 72 - 48 - (-48 + 96 + 288) = 72
\]

Result:
\[
\left|
    \begin{array}{ccc}
    12 & 6 & -4\\
    6 & 4 & 4\\
    3 & 2 & 8
    \end{array}
\right| = 72
\]
